A **Tesla wrap visualizer** helps because Tesla styling is simple on purpose. Small changes in finish, trim, and wheel contrast show up more clearly than on many other cars.

## Popular Tesla directions

- Satin gray or stealth tones
- Clean monochrome builds with black trim
- Strong contrast wheel and brake accent setups

## What to review before deciding

1. Color finish in bright light
2. Wheel contrast with the wrap
3. Chrome delete or trim blackout consistency

## Why preview first

Minimal body lines make finish choice more important. What looks subtle on another car can look flat or perfect on a Tesla depending on the combination.
