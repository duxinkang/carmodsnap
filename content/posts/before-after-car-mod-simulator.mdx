A **before and after car mod simulator** helps you evaluate visual impact before committing budget. It is the simplest way to decide whether a change is meaningful or just different.

## What to measure in stock vs modified comparisons

- Improvement in style coherence
- Visual impact per dollar spent
- Compatibility with future upgrades

## Decision model

1. Save current baseline as "before"
2. Build one focused "after" concept
3. Add one alternative "after" concept
4. Keep the option with best value-to-impact ratio

## Why buyers use this approach

It prevents impulse part purchases and helps you communicate a clear plan to installers.
