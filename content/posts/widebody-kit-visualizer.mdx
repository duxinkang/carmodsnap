A **widebody kit visualizer** helps you check whether a wider, more aggressive setup looks intentional or overdone. That decision is hard to judge from standalone part photos.

## What to compare in a widebody preview

- Fender width vs wheel offset
- Ride height vs body aggression
- Whether the car still has visual balance from the side profile

## When widebody works best

Widebody styling usually needs support from wheels, stance, and trim direction. Previewing one part without the others leads to bad decisions.

## Budget reality

Widebody projects rarely stop at one part. Preview first so you understand whether the full direction is worth the total spend.
