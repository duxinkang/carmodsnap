A **PPF vs wrap visualizer** helps when you are choosing between two very different outcomes: surface protection versus stronger visual transformation.

## The core difference

- **PPF** is usually about preserving paint and adding subtle finish changes
- **Wrap** is about stronger color and style changes

## When to choose PPF

Choose PPF when your factory paint already works and you mainly want protection or a slight satin effect.

## When to choose wrap

Choose wrap when you want a bigger identity change, stronger contrast, or a completely different color direction.

## Decision shortcut

If the goal is protection first, start with PPF. If the goal is visual change first, start with wrap.
