A **car livery design tool** is useful when you need to test more than color. Once graphics, stripe direction, and sponsor placement enter the picture, layout clarity matters as much as style.

## What a livery preview should validate

- Stripe direction follows the car shape
- Logos stay readable at speed and distance
- Main graphics do not fight wheel or body details

## Best use cases

- Track-day liveries
- Brand campaign vehicles
- Drift and motorsport-inspired street builds

## Why preview before print

Graphics production gets expensive fast when alignment is wrong. A preview catches layout problems before install day.
