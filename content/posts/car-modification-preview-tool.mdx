A **car modification preview tool** turns random ideas into a structured build plan. If your next step is buying parts, preview first so your budget goes to changes you actually want.

## Why preview-first planning works

- You remove low-confidence options early
- You align style direction before spending
- You can share clearer references with installers

## Practical framework for planning

1. Define your target look in one sentence
2. Build three versions: safe, balanced, aggressive
3. Evaluate each version against your budget
4. Move forward with the best value-to-impact option

## What this improves commercially

You reduce unnecessary part swaps and reduce quote back-and-forth with shops. That means faster decisions and fewer sunk costs.
