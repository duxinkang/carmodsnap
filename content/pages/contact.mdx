## Contact CarModSnap

CarModSnap is an AI-powered car modification visualization service for digital concept previews. We do not sell physical car parts, wraps, wheels, body kits, or installation services through this website.

For support, billing, refund, account, privacy, or product questions, contact us by email:

**admin@carmodsnap.com**

## Support Scope

We can help with:

- Account access and sign-in issues
- Billing, invoices, subscription cancellation, and refund requests
- Credit balance or generation history questions
- Problems uploading vehicle images or creating AI previews
- Privacy, data deletion, or content removal requests
- Reports about inaccurate claims, demo content, or unclear product information

## Response Time

We usually reply within **1-3 business days**. Billing or refund requests should include the email used at checkout and the order ID or transaction ID from your receipt.
