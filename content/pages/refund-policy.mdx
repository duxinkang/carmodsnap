## Overview

This Refund Policy explains how CarModSnap handles subscription cancellations and refund requests.

## Subscription Cancellation

- You can cancel an active subscription at any time from **Settings > Billing**.
- If you cancel, the subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period unless otherwise required by law.
- We do not charge additional cancellation fees.

## Refund Eligibility

- Charges are generally non-refundable after a billing cycle starts.
- Refunds may be issued where required by applicable consumer protection laws.
- If a duplicate charge or clear billing error occurs, contact us and we will investigate promptly.

## Trials and Promotional Offers

- Trial and promotional terms, if provided, are shown on the pricing or checkout pages.
- Unless explicitly stated, trial conversions and promotion-based purchases follow the same cancellation and refund rules above.

## Chargebacks and Disputes

- If you believe a payment is unauthorized or incorrect, contact us first so we can help resolve it quickly.
- You may also raise a dispute through your payment provider according to their policies.
