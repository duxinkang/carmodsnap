## Purpose

This policy explains how **ModSnap** presents product claims, metrics, testimonials, and partner information on our website.  
Our goal is to avoid misleading statements and keep all public-facing claims accurate and reviewable.

## Data Sources and Claim Standards

- We only publish factual claims that can be reasonably substantiated by internal records or verifiable public data.
- If data is unavailable, incomplete, or in testing, we use neutral language and avoid quantified growth claims.
- Marketing copy is periodically reviewed and updated when product scope changes.

## Demo and Sample Content

- Some screens may include **demo/sample data** for UI preview.
- Demo data is explicitly labeled (for example, "Demo Data" or "Sample concept (demo)").
- Demo content is not presented as verified customer activity, merchant endorsements, or real transaction history.

## Testimonials and Social Proof

- We do not publish fabricated customer testimonials as real customer feedback.
- Any quote, review, or case-study style content must be either:
  1. from a real user with permission, or
  2. clearly marked as sample/demo content.

## Ratings, Rankings, and Merchant Profiles

- Rating values, review counts, and certification labels are shown only when backed by actual data.
- Fallback/demo merchant records are marked as demo and avoid synthetic trust signals.

## Pricing and Product Claims

- Plan details, billing terms, and feature descriptions are published on the pricing and legal pages.
- We avoid guaranteed outcome language and do not claim unsupported integrations, endorsements, or market position.
